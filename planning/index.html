    Planning

    With the knowledge you gathered in the first phase you are ready to start turning that into an actionable plan. We wrote this section to guide you through the process of making that plan. A solid architecture prepares you for success in development and beyond.

    On this page:

    1. Mapping Requirements
    2. Picking a project type and development model
    3. Planning content management
    4. Identifying data structures

    There are four main components to planning the projects architecture:

    Mapping Requirements

    Take business requirements and identify the suitable Kentico features and functionalities.

    Picking a project type and development model

    Choose the appropriate development model.

    Planning content management

    Create a strategy for the initial and ongoing content and asset production, organization, etc.

    Identifying data structures

    Build an information architecture to act as a foundation for development.
